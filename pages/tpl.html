Tronic Premiere League

TPL is one of the biggest sport event organized by the Electronic Club that brings the department community together via a friendly cricket matches. TPL has been one of the traditional events in the E-clubs calendar each year where undergraduates of all the levels in the department are brought to one level and strengthen the friendship among them.

Although the main goal of this event was to ameliorate the bond between the undergraduates the academic staff has been joining to play an exhibition match for past few years.
